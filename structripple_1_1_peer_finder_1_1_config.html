int ripple::PeerFinder::Config::maxPeers

The largest number of public peer slots to allow.

This includes both inbound and outbound, but does not include fixed peers.

double ripple::PeerFinder::Config::outPeers

The number of automatic outbound connections to maintain.

Outbound connections are only maintained if autoConnect is true. The value can be fractional; The decision to round up or down will be made using a per-process pseudorandom number and a probability proportional to the fractional part. Example: If outPeers is 9.3, then 30% of nodes will maintain 9 outbound connections, while 70% of nodes will maintain 10 outbound connections.
