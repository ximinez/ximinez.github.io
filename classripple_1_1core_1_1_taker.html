Amounts ripple::core::Taker::remaining_offer ( ) const

Returns the amount remaining on the offer.

This is the amount at which the offer should be placed. It may either be for the full amount when there were no crossing offers, or for zero when the offer fully crossed, or any amount in between. It is always at the original offer quality (m_quality)
